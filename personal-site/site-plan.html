Home page

My website is a portfolio for showcasing my skills and knowledge of music and programming. There will be a page showcasing my programming skills, including the websites that I have made for this class and any other programming projects I have done. There will be a link to my GitHub. There will be another page for showcasing the music I have written, and I will include behind-the-scenes and general info about my music-making process. There will be a link to my YouTube channel, SoundCloud, and Bandcamp. The Home page will be a general about me with links to the other pages. The primary audience of this page is recruiters and employers, but it can also be for anyone interested in my work.

About me: Hi, I'm Andrew! I enjoy programming and writing music. I've been learning programming since I was 12 and writing music since I was 15. I am currently at BYUI in Rexburg Idaho studying Software Engineering.

My Music

This page will include links to my music and info about my experience writing music and my process.

Besides being good with computers, I am also good with music. When I was just seven years old, my mother started me on piano lessons; this is when I developed a love for music. When I was 15, I wrote and published my first song on my YouTube channel. I wrote it in GarageBand on my iPod Touch using the jazz music theory I learned from piano lessons. I have also taken up the Organ, drums, and guitar. Since then, I have continued to learn, experiment, and expand my ever-growing music catalog. As of writing this, I have written 33 songs and three EPs, but there is still lots more to come. The software I use to write my music is FL Studio Producers Edition, a DAW (digital audio workstation). I use a Yamaha MODX 7 as my main MIDI keyboard alongside a KORG Minilogue XD. I specialize in a specific genre of electronic music called synthwave, which involves capturing the sound of the 80's using drum machines and synthesizers. I use the Arturia V collection 9 and serum as my main synths. I publish my music on my YouTube channel, Soundcloud, and Bandcamp.

My Projects

This page will include my portfolio of programming skills, including the websites I have made for this class and the other things I have made. There will be a link to my GitHub and LinkedIn. I will also provide my experience as a programmer for those interested.

I have always been interested in computers, whether that be how they work or how the games I like playing were made. I was introduced to the world of computers and computer programming at the age of 12 when I received a Raspberry Pi 3 Model B for Christmas. The Raspberry Pi 3 Model B is a credit card-size computer that runs Linux and is used for home servers, emulation, and robotics, to name a few. I used it as a way to learn how to program using a book about Python and a version of Minecraft explicitly made for modification. When I was 15, I started making games on the popular game platform ROBLOX using the programming language Lua. I currently know how to program in Python, Lua, and Javascript, and I am learning C++. I am studying software engineering at BYUI, Idaho, and learning to program games as a hobby. Any game I have made will be included here, and the source code will be available to be viewed on my GitHub, but I can't promise it will be readable.
